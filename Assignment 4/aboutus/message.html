PRESIDENT MESSAGE

FANoFAN Industry has overcome the test of time. We have come a long way from out humble beginnings to a highly integrated organization. We pride ourselves on our proven track record stretching to over 60 years for providing quality products.


The products of FANoFAN Home Appliances are symbol of elegance, quality, efficiency and durability as we believe in maintaining the long term relationships with our customers. Nothing is more important than the expectation of the consumers of FANoFAN brand. We are committed and dedicated to provide best quality products to our customers.


We consistently upgrade our systems and apply modern techniques to meet the ever growing expectations of our customers more professionally. Being market leader, we held ourselves responsible for delivering state of the art products. Our workforce is distinguished by their technical expertise coupled with their hands-on-experience, thereby ensuring that our customers receive the best quality products. That is what we have done in the past and that is the reason the company is enjoying the major market share in the country for so many years and making its international impact as well.


In the end, I would like to thank all the employees and consumers of FANoFAN brand, who have contributed much in making us the successful organization as it is today. Moreover, I am looking forward to serve our new customers with the hope that we can build a long lasting relationship with them too based on complete trust.
