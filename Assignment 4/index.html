Welcome To FANoFAN


FANoFAN Established in 1954, is one of the largest manufacturers of high quality electric fans in Pakistan.We have an extensive distribution network all over the Globe.


For our persistent performance in Exports and for our superior brand recognition in the local market, FANoFAN was the first company to receive the Export Trophy and Brand of the Year Awards in the fan Industry of Pakistan. Ever since, FANoFAN has continued to be the recipient of numerous Awards for the acknowledgement of our foremost commitment to producing and exporting high quality fans.


Furthermore, FANoFAN is also the first ever company in Pakistan to be certified by UL (Underwriter Laboratories, USA) and CE to export fans in USA, Canada and EU


With FANoFAN products you do not have to compromise on either efficiency or aesthetics. Due to high quality materials, our products are durable and are as tough on the inside as they are beautiful on the outside


We guarantee to deliver smooth, cooling comfort through our diligently crafted beautiful products that transform your environment.


Our wide range of products ensure you find exactly what you need.
